Same trader, same thesis, same day.

Secondary lessons:

- The tilt moment was real: down $900, with a third short pre-staged. The discipline that saved the day (limit entries, ratcheted stops, taking the fill) only showed up *after* the revenge trade — the tilt-guard app idea earned its build slot today.
